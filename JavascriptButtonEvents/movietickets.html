Create a Movie ticket cost calculator.

  1. Create 2 Text boxes in a page with labels Age, number of tickets
  2. Create a button to calculate the final cost of movie tickets
  3. Enter the age and number of tickets in the text boxes
  4. The result should be displayed in the Result text box created below the Calculate button

These are the prices for each tickets.

  • Children under 3 years old - Free
  • Children between 3 and 12 years old - $10
  • Adults between 13 and 60 years old - $15
  • Seniors above 60 years old - $12
